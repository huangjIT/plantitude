Delivery Information

1. Free shipping to most areas in the US (additional shipping fee required for remote areas such as Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico; contact customer service for details);
2. All potted plants are strictly inspected before shipment to ensure plant health;
3. Orders are shipped within 48 hours after placement, default SF Express, estimated delivery in 2-5 days.

After-sales Guarantee

1. If plants are severely damaged/wilted within 24 hours of receipt, contact customer service for return/exchange;
2. For plant issues caused by improper personal care, free care guidance is provided, returns/exchanges are not supported;
3. For returns/exchanges, retain original packaging and intact plants, take photos for evidence and contact customer service for processing.

Payment Methods

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay. Unpaid orders will be automatically canceled within 1 hour after placement.
