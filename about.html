About Us

Green Isle - Born from Passion, Rooted in Nature

Founded in 2018, Green Isle was established by a group of young people who love plants and life. We have deep expertise in potted plant cultivation, selecting high-quality seedlings from the source and adhering to pesticide-free, pollution-free cultivation methods to bring healthy, fresh potted plants to our customers.

We believe that a small potted plant can bring healing and warmth to the fast-paced modern life. Whether it's a mini succulent for your desk or a large green plant for your living room, it's a beautiful connection between nature and life.

In the future, we will continue to uphold our original aspiration, expand our product range, and improve service quality to let more people experience the beauty of life brought by potted plants.
